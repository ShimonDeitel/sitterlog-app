Privacy Policy

Babysitter Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information.

The app makes no network requests. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed from your device.
